CREDIT MANAGEMENT

Since inception, Management Services has provided verification’s and collections service solutions to its numerous clients and helped them grow business, become more profitable and control over dues.

We assist clients in analyzing the credit worthiness of their potential customers by means of physical verification and defaulter check.

We currently provide Verification and Field Support in the area of:

  • Company Profiling
  • Customer / Individual Verification
  • PRE and POST Loan Disbursement Verification
  • Know Your Customer
  • Vendor Verification

We help our clients assess credit/product worthiness of their customers through:

  • Stringent Physical Verification
  • Neighbor and Reference Check
  • Document Check
  • Defaulter Check from our huge pool of information

We have an internal audit team that makes random calls to customer to keep a check on quality of physical verification. All calls are recorded and monitored from time to time.

Periodical training’s are conducted to uphold verification quality and code of conduct.

CUSTOMER SERVICES

We specialize in soft collections and are currently managing largest Bucket-X and Bucket-1 allocations PAN India for clients like HDFC Bank, Kotak Prime for various products.

Soft Collections:

We provide tele-calling and field support for flows collection that include First bill default, Bucket-X and Bucket-1:

  • Make reminder calls/Mails/SMS/Visits to the customers for their dues
  • Encourage customers to make payments directly for their dues
  • Arrange for pickups
  • Assist customers with billing and service issues
  • Help customers to resolve their issues with clients

Domestic Calls Support:

We provide tele-calling support for value added services and other services of our clients. Currently, the tele-calling setup includes:

  • Appointment Fixing: Outbound calls to generate appointments for pick-up process
  • Awareness Calls: Go Green (calls to promote bills through email over paper bills) and ECS (Calls to promote Electronic bill clearing services)
  • Inbound call support to manage leads generated from marketing activities conducted by our clients
  • Technical Support: Technical support provided to customers of our telecom clients to help them use services effectively

Customer Relationship Management:

  • We enhance the experience of clients customers by means of courtesy calls, awareness calls and welcome visits
  • We execute marketing campaigns of clients through SMS, Bulk Emails, Pamphlet Distribution and Canopies
  • We provide clients with detailed analysis of customer interactions and feedback to assist them in formulating marketing and selling strategies
